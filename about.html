About My Application

This application is designed to integrate Gradescope assignments seamlessly with your Google Calendar. By connecting your Gradescope account, the app will automatically sync your assignments, due dates, and deadlines directly to your Google Calendar. This ensures that you never miss an important assignment or deadline again.

Key Features:

  • Automatic synchronization of Gradescope assignments with Google Calendar
  • Real-time updates and notifications
  • Easy setup and configuration
  • Customizable reminders and alerts
  • User-friendly interface

This app is the perfect tool to help you manage your academic schedule efficiently.

How to Use

  1. Download the correct executable for your system
  2. Run the executable in the folder
  3. Log into your accounts (google and gradescope)
  4. Done! The Google Grade Scope Assignment Calendar Sync App (GGSACSA) will now start syncing your Assignments to your google Calendar every couple hours while your system is on
