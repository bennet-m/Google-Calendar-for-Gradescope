Account Setup for SSO Users

All gradescope accounts can sign in with an email and password associated with the account. If your school uses “School Credentials” to sign in, you need to set up your password for your Gradescope account in order to use GradeSync.

  1. Go to https://www.gradescope.com/login
  2. Click “Forgot your password?”
  3. Then, use the school email associated with your Gradescope account and follow the email prompts to set your password
  4. Now use these credentials in GradeSync to connect your account
