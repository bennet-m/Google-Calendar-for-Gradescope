Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we handle and protect your personal information when you use our application designed to integrate Gradescope assignments with your Google Calendar.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data from you. Our application accesses your Gradescope assignment data but does not store or collect it. The data processing all happens on your local device.

Cookies

To enhance your experience and ensure you do not have to log in to Gradescope repeatedly, we save your login cookies. These cookies are stored locally on your device and are used solely to maintain your session with Gradescope.

Use of Your Data

- Cookies: The login cookies are used to keep you logged in to Gradescope. We do not use these cookies for any other purpose.
- Gradescope Assignment Data: While our application accesses your Gradescope assignment data to sync with your Google Calendar, this data is not collected, stored, or used for any other purpose.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your data. The cookies are stored securely on your device, and no personal data is transmitted or stored on our servers.

Your Consent

By using our application, you consent to our privacy policy.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
