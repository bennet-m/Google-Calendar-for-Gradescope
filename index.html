What does GradeSync do?

This application is designed to integrate Gradescope assignments seamlessly with your Google Calendar. By connecting your Gradescope account, the app will automatically sync your assignments, due dates, and deadlines directly to your Google Calendar. This ensures that you never miss an important assignment or deadline again.

How to set it up (Mac)

  1. Download here

  2. Open the app. If you see this warning, it's because the app is not from the App Store. Open the app by right-clicking on it and pressing "Open." You might have to do this step twice.

  3. If you want the app to run automatically, accept the popup asking to grant GradeSync access to administer your computer. If you disallow access, you'll have to run GradeSync yourself when you want your calendar to update.
  4. Sign in to your Google account when prompted.
  5. Sign into your Gradescope account when prompted. If you usually log in with "School Credentials" and don't go to Harvey Mudd, go here to enable logging in to Gradescope with email and password.
Done! Your Gradescope assignments will now be synced to your Google Calendar.

How to set it up (Windows)

  1. Download here
  2. Extract the downloaded zip file.
  3. Run the executable inside as administrator. Right click on the extracted executable and click run as administrator.
  4. Sign in to your Google account when prompted.
  5. Sign into your Gradescope account when prompted. If you usually log in with "School Credentials" and don't attend Harvey Mudd College, go here to enable Gradescope loggin via email and password.

Administrator privledge is only needed to ensure your calendar updates automatically; it will not accsess your data ( Privacy Policy ). You may also choose to not give Admin privledges but you will need run the executable manually to update your calendar.

Done! Your Gradescope assignments will now be synced to your Google Calendar.
